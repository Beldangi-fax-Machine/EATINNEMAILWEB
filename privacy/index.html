Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026 | Version 2.0

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how EAT-INN ("the App"), operated by Pokhrel Development LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

1

Information We Collect

Account Information (Identifiers)

  • Apple ID identifier (via Sign in with Apple)
  • Email address (if provided during sign-up or via Apple relay)
  • Display name (optional)

User-Generated Content

  • Recipes you create, import, or save
  • Kitchen inventory items and grocery lists
  • Meal plans and nutrition tracking data
  • Dietary preferences, allergies, and restrictions
  • Photos of food you upload for AI analysis

Commercial Information

  • Subscription status and purchase history (managed by Apple)
  • Token balance for ad-based rewards

Internet/Electronic Activity

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device type, operating system version
  • Error logs and crash reports
  • IP address (for security and rate limiting)

Inferences

  • Food preferences based on saved recipes
  • Dietary patterns from meal tracking

We do not collect precise geolocation data, biometric data, or sensitive personal information beyond dietary preferences.

2

Sources of Information

We collect information from the following sources:

  • Directly from you: When you create an account, add recipes, upload photos, or configure preferences
  • Automatically: When you use the App, we collect usage data and device information
  • Apple Sign-In: Basic account identifiers when you authenticate
  • Third-party services: Subscription status from Apple/RevenueCat; error data from crash reporting
3

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain App functionality
  • Generate personalized recipe and meal suggestions using AI
  • Analyze food photos to identify ingredients and suggest recipes
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Process subscriptions and manage your account
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send notifications (only if you enable them)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations
4

AI & Image Processing

EAT-INN uses artificial intelligence to enhance your experience:

  • AI Provider: We use OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini model for recipe generation, ingredient analysis, and food photo recognition
  • How it works: When you upload a food photo or request recipe suggestions, the image or text is sent to OpenAI's servers for processing
  • Data handling: OpenAI processes data according to their API data usage policy. As of our implementation, OpenAI does not use API data for training
  • No advertising use: Your images are never sold or used for advertising purposes
  • No public sharing: Images are not publicly shared unless you explicitly choose to share content

Automated Decision-Making: AI suggestions are recommendations only. You always have final control over recipes, meal plans, and dietary choices. No automated decisions with legal or significant effects are made without human involvement.

5

Data Storage & Security

  • Primary storage: Your data is stored securely using Supabase cloud infrastructure (servers located in the United States)
  • Encryption: We use encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access controls: Data access is restricted to authorized systems using role-based permissions
  • Security monitoring: We use Sentry for error monitoring to quickly identify and fix issues

No system is 100% secure. We follow industry-standard security practices and will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering any data breach affecting personal information.

6

Third-Party Services

EAT-INN uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase (supabase.com) - Database hosting and authentication infrastructure
  • Apple - Sign in with Apple authentication, App Store payments, StoreKit subscriptions
  • RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) - Subscription management and purchase validation
  • OpenAI (openai.com) - AI-powered recipe generation and food image analysis
  • Google AdMob (admob.google.com) - Rewarded video ads for earning tokens (free tier users)
  • Sentry (sentry.io) - Error monitoring and crash reporting

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

7

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months.

We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: With the third-party services listed above, solely to operate the App
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of users or the public
  • Your choice: If you choose to share recipes publicly with other users
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (you will be notified)

Categories of Third Parties

In the past 12 months, we have disclosed personal information to:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (Supabase)
  • Payment processors (Apple)
  • AI service providers (OpenAI)
  • Advertising networks (Google AdMob - for users who watch rewarded ads)
  • Analytics and error monitoring services (Sentry)
8

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of notifications at any time in Settings

Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly in the App:

  • Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Confirm deletion when prompted
  • Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted

You may also request deletion by emailing support@eatinn.org.

9

California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

Your Rights

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information collected, used, and disclosed in the past 12 months
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: We do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: We only use sensitive information (dietary preferences) as necessary to provide the App
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising purposes.

How to Exercise Your Rights

To submit a request, email support@eatinn.org with subject line "CCPA Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days.

12-Month Lookback

See Section 1 (Information We Collect) and Section 7 (Data Sharing) for categories of personal information collected and disclosed in the preceding 12 months.

10

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

Lawful Basis for Processing

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the App services you requested
  • Consent: For optional features like notifications and marketing (you can withdraw consent anytime)
  • Legitimate interests: For security, fraud prevention, and service improvement
  • Legal obligation: When required by law

Your GDPR Rights

  • Access: Obtain confirmation and a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Automated decisions: Not be subject to solely automated decisions with legal effects

International Data Transfers

Your data is transferred to and processed in the United States. We protect international transfers using:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Data processing agreements with our service providers

Data Protection Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

Contact for EU Inquiries

Email: support@eatinn.org with subject line "GDPR Request"

11

Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Account deletion: Upon deletion request, account data is permanently removed within 30 days
  • Backup purge: Backups containing deleted data are purged within 90 days
  • Legal retention: Some data may be retained longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention)
  • Anonymized data: We may retain anonymized, aggregated data indefinitely for analytics
12

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

  • EAT-INN is not intended for children under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly
  • If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact support@eatinn.org
13

Teen Users (Ages 13-17)

If you are between 13 and 17 years old:

  • You may use EAT-INN with permission from a parent or legal guardian
  • We recommend parents review this Privacy Policy with their teens
  • Parents can request access to or deletion of their teen's data by contacting us
  • We do not knowingly sell or share personal information of users under 16 for targeted advertising

Additional protections apply under state laws including California (CCPA), Connecticut, Virginia, and Vermont.
