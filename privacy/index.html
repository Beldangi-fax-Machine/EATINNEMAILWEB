Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how EAT-INN ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

1

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Apple ID (via Sign in with Apple)
  • Email address (if provided)

User Content

  • Recipes you create or save
  • Kitchen inventory items
  • Preferences and settings

Usage Data

  • App interactions
  • Features used
  • App performance data

We do not collect precise location data.

2

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain App functionality
  • Personalize recipe and meal suggestions
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send notifications (only if you enable them)
3

AI & Image Processing

  • Photos of food are processed to generate recipe suggestions.
  • Images are not sold or used for advertising.
  • Images are not publicly shared unless you choose to share content.
4

Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored securely using Supabase cloud services.
  • We use encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Access is restricted to authorized systems only.

No system is 100% secure, but we follow industry-standard practices.

5

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the App (e.g., hosting, analytics)
  • When required by law
  • If you choose to share recipes publicly with other users
6

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of notifications at any time

To request deletion, contact support@eatinn.org.

7

Data Retention

  • Data is retained only as long as your account is active or as required for legal purposes.
  • Deleted accounts result in permanent data removal within a reasonable timeframe.
8

Children's Privacy

  • EAT-INN is not intended for children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.
9

Third-Party Services

EAT-INN uses trusted third-party services for:

  • Authentication
  • Payments
  • Cloud storage
  • Error monitoring

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and security obligations.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11

Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests:

Email: support@eatinn.org
