Delete Your Data

How to request data deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. Follow the instructions below to permanently remove your data from EAT-INN.

1

Delete Your Account via the App

The easiest way to delete your account and all associated data is directly within the EAT-INN app:

  1. Open the EAT-INN app on your device
  2. Navigate to Settings (tap the gear icon or your profile)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted
  5. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted
2

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account information and profile
  • All recipes you have created or saved
  • Your kitchen inventory items
  • Preferences and app settings
  • Any usage history and analytics data

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure to export any recipes you want to keep before deleting your account.

3

Alternative: Contact Support

If you are unable to access the app or need assistance with data deletion, you can contact us directly:

Email: support@eatinn.org

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

4

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • Your data is permanently removed from our active systems
  • Backup data may be retained for up to 30 days before full deletion
  • We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you
  • Certain data may be kept if required by law
5

Questions?

If you have any questions about data deletion or your privacy rights, please review our Privacy Policy or contact us:

Email: support@eatinn.org
