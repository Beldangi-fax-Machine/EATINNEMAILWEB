Frequently Asked Questions

What is Eatinn?
Eatinn is your personal meal planning and food discovery companion. We help you find delicious meals, plan your week, and make eating well easier than ever.
How do I reset my password?
Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password. The link expires after 24 hours.
Is Eatinn free to use?
Yes! Eatinn offers a free tier with core features. Premium features are available with our subscription plans for users who want an enhanced experience.
How do I delete my account?
You can delete your account from the app settings under "Account" ‚Üí "Delete Account". Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
I didn't receive my verification email
Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there, you can request a new verification email from the app. Make sure you entered the correct email address.
