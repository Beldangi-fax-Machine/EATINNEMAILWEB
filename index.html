FAQ

What is Eat-Inn?
Eat-Inn is your personal meal planning companion. Find delicious meals from ingredients you already have, reducing food waste and making cooking effortless.
Is Eat-Inn available on all devices?
Eat-Inn works on iOS and Android. We're continuously expanding platform availability.
How do I reset my password?
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Enter your email and we'll send a reset link that expires in 24 hours.
Is Eat-Inn free to use?
Yes! Core features are free. Premium features are available via subscription.
What should I do if I encounter issues?
Contact support@eatinn.org with details about the problem and your device.
I didn't receive my verification email
Check your spam folder. You can request a new verification email from the app.
