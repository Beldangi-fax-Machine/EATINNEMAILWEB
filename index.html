FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Eat-Inn?
+
Eat-Inn is your personal meal planning and food discovery companion. We help you find delicious meals from what's already in your kitchen, reducing food waste and making cooking effortless.
Is Eat-Inn available on all devices?
+
Eat-Inn is designed for mobile devices and works seamlessly on both iOS and Android. We're continuously working on expanding our platform availability.
How do I reset my password?
+
Go to the login screen and tap "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires after 24 hours for security.
Is Eat-Inn free to use?
+
Yes! Eat-Inn offers a free tier with core features. Premium features are available with our subscription plans for users who want an enhanced culinary experience.
What should I do if I encounter issues?
+
If you experience any issues, please contact our support team at support@eatinn.org. Include details about the problem and your device, and we'll help resolve it as quickly as possible.
I didn't receive my verification email
+
Check your spam/junk folder first. If it's not there, you can request a new verification email from the app. Make sure you entered the correct email address during signup.

Still need help?

Our support team is here for you. Reach out and we'll help you as soon as possible.
